Research Area

ACC Lab research mainly focuses on the application of both AI and network optimization in wireless and networking system. Currently, we are interested in the following topics.


  • AI/Machine Learning Applications.
  • Network Security and Privacy.
  • Mobile Computing, Data Analytics.
  • Wireless Communications, 5G, 6G, Internet of Things.
  • Communications and Networks: Ambient backscatter, intelligent surfaces, 6G, edge/fog computing, IoT and vehicles networks.
  • Applications of Machine Learning: Reinforcement learning, deep reinforcement learning, distributed deep learning and federated learning.
  • Security: Physical layer security, cyberattack detection, blockchain and adversarial machine learning.
  • Optimization: Operations research, control theory, game theory, and queuing theory.
