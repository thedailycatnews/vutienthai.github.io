Agile Communications and Computing Group

Agile Communications and Computing Group is in School of Electrical and Data Engineering at the University of Technology Sydney and is led by Senior Lecturer Diep N. Nguyen.

Our research spans a broad range of topics which are mainly categorized into follwing branches:
  1. AI/Machine Learning Applications
  2. Network Security and Privacy
  3. Mobile Computing, Data Analytics
  4. Wireless Communications, 5G, 6G, Internet of Things
  5. Network Economics
  6. Game Theory
  7. Algorithm and Protocol Design
Our recent research interests are in the areas of computer networking, mobile computing, cyber security, wireless communications (5G/6G), and machine learning/AI application, with emphasis on systems' performance and security/privacy.
